Happy Birthday Mummy...

In all our years!
From the first breath to present
From birth to our still growing years
Through all the stages of our lives, yet seen
You never ceased to show your LOVE
You always gave your shoulders for us to cry on
You remained the greatest supporter of all our goals

Days where we were dragged to prayers
Days where your words brought comfort, dispensed knowledge and your admonition brought direction
Days where your contagious laughter, smile, dance and heart brightened up the room
Days where intimate conversations made us heard
For this and many more we say THANK YOU MUMMY…. We cannot but LOVE our NNE!
